Sponsorship

In order to complete a vehicle every year, we rely on donations and sponsorship from local, national, and international partners. We focus our expenses on necessary components like the frame, registration, tires, materials, and any improvements that yield the largest benefit for their cost. Though we have a lower budget than other top tier teams, we are able to continually improve our designs as we receive more contributions and as we improve as experienced students.

We would like to thank all of our current sponsors, and any new sponsors for your contributions. By sponsoring our team, you not only allow us to continue to do what we love, but also allow the team to reach new levels. Each year we promote an experienced team who develop into industry leading engineers. All of this would not be possible without your support.

If you are interested in partnering with UVic Formula Motorsport, please contact us at uvic.fsae (at) gmail.com
